# The Build-Up to Launch: Security, Compliance, and Control

In the final days before launch, we prioritized trust and control across the entire platform.

## Core milestones we completed

- **Role and account boundaries**: policy-driven account access controls across forms, inbox, notes, replies, and insights.
- **Stricter protection defaults**: deny-by-default route guarding and explicit coverage for protected navigation paths.
- **Compliance workflows**: DSR actions (export/delete/restrict), retention cleanup, anonymization, and audit evidence trails.
- **Admin safeguards**: least-privilege controls, break-glass approvals, verification enforcement, and session hardening.
- **PII minimization**: reduced sensitive data in logs and notifications with stronger rendering and security checks.

## Impact for teams

These updates gave teams more confidence to run customer-facing operations in a system designed with accountability and data stewardship in mind.
