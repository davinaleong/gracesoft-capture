# 24 Hours to Launch: Product Polish and Readiness

The day before launch was all about confidence.

We focused on the details that determine whether a product feels dependable in real operations, not just in demos.

## Highlights from the final day

- **Guided onboarding coherence**: unified dashboard onboarding sidebar with panel support flow.
- **Public safety and clarity**: hardened navigation and added legal/help pages for cleaner first impressions.
- **Inbox consistency improvements**: refined timeline and reply behavior for smoother day-to-day communication.
- **Billing visibility fixes**: corrected subscription sync and dashboard plan reflection so account state stays trustworthy.
- **Demo funnel support**: added an ephemeral free-plan capture flow to reduce friction for new users.

## Why this mattered before launch

Customers evaluate reliability quickly. These updates made the first session clearer, safer, and more predictable, exactly when it mattered most.
