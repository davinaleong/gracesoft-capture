# From Beta to Billing-Ready in One Focused Sprint

April 1 marked a major transition for GraceSoft Capture.

In a single sprint window, we shipped the commercial and operational foundations needed to support real customer teams.

## Milestones completed

- **Stripe billing foundation**: product/price sync, secure webhook verification, and idempotency handling.
- **Owner-safe purchase flows**: owner-only checkout and billing portal controls.
- **Checkout quality improvements**: iterative fixes to make upgrade flow clearer and more reliable.
- **Workspace provisioning**: billing-connected setup path for smoother conversion from trial to active usage.
- **Go-to-market surface**: a dedicated landing page and polished support email styling.

## What this unlocked

These milestones connected product value to operational readiness, allowing GraceSoft Capture to launch with both a functional core and a sustainable growth path.
