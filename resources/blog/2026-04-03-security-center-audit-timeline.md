# GraceSoft Capture Is Live: Launched Yesterday

Yesterday, we officially launched GraceSoft Capture.

This release was the result of a rapid, milestone-driven build cycle that turned a simple capture concept into a secure, production-ready customer operations platform.

## What we shipped before launch

- **Billing foundation and checkout reliability**: Stripe-ready billing, secure webhook verification, idempotency handling, and owner-only checkout/portal flows were completed and hardened.
- **Public-facing readiness**: landing experience, legal/help pages, and SEO/social metadata were added so customers can discover and evaluate the product clearly.
- **Operational onboarding**: dashboard onboarding and panel support flows were unified to reduce first-run confusion and improve activation.
- **Inbox and reply stability**: timeline and reply behavior were refined so customer communication feels predictable and fast.

## Why this launch matters

GraceSoft Capture was built to help teams stop losing context across forms, support requests, and customer replies. The launch marks a big step toward giving offices one dependable place to capture, route, and respond.

## What comes next

Now that launch is complete, our focus shifts to iterative improvements informed by real customer usage, with continued work on insights, reliability, and team workflow speed.

Thanks for building with us.

- Timeline entries now show clearer status labels for request, approval, and completion events.
- Event timestamps are grouped by day to reduce scanning fatigue on long histories.
- Quick filters let you narrow the timeline to break-glass and DSR actions.

## Why this matters

Teams performing regular audits can now move through evidence review with less friction and fewer missed events.
